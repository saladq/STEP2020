About Me

Since high school, I have been taking courses in CS but it wasn't until college where I really started to get into it. As of fall 2019, I declared my concentration in Computer Science and look forward to taking more classes perhaps in articial intelligence and design. As of now, I have taken basic courses in functional programming, data structures and algorithms, and theoretical CS. I am currently a Google STEP intern for the summer of 2020 where I have been working remotely but still getting to know my awesome teammates.

On campus I am mainly involved in AADT (Asian American Dance Troup), Breakers, and The Crimson as a web and print designer. In my spare time, I like to swim, crochet, and bake. My home is based in New York City where I frequently go into the city to catch up with friends over meals or by meandering around.

Hobbies

I love swimming, playing volleyball, and playing badminton. I also love doing a lot of small artworks on the side whenever I have free time. These tend to be pretty unconventional. I like making pottery, crocheting and baking. And among these interests, I probably like baking the most, specifically bread and cakes. I gained an interest in baking the winter of 2018-2019. There really is nothing like the satisfaction of kneading dough to a smooth, milky complexion and of eating a whole-days-effort three layer cake topped with tons of whipped cream and fruits.
